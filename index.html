Granite Mountain

Arizona National Park

Memorial State Park

We invite you to hike the trail to better understand the experience of these men as well as to appreciate the beauty of the town of Yarnell and the surrounding areas. Hike the Hotshots Trail from the parking lot trailhead up to the overlook where you'll see sweeping views in every direction, and perhaps leave a memento on our remembrance wall. The hike is approximately 3.5 miles long from the trailhead to the Fatality Site, for a full length of about 7 miles.
